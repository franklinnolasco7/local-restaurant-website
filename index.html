About Us

For the past 8 years, we have been providing affordable foods.

Welcome to our Filipino panciteria! We are a family-owned restaurant that has been serving delicious and authentic Filipino cuisine for several years. Our mission is to provide our customers with a warm and welcoming dining experience while offering a diverse menu that showcases the rich flavors of Filipino cuisine.

Try some of our Filipino cuisine!

Our menu features a variety of popular Filipino dishes. We serve traditional Filipino dishes such as adobo, sinigang, sisig, and various types of pancit, but our specialty is the Pancit Habhab and lumpiang shanghai, which have a distinct flavor that will satisfy your taste buds.

Pork Adobo
